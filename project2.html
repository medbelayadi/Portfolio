OverSee

OverSee is a comprehensive leave management application designed to streamline the process of managing employee time off. It provides a user-friendly interface that allows employees to easily request leave, track their leave balance, and view the status of their requests in real-time. OverSee also offers robust features for managers, enabling them to approve or deny leave requests, view team calendars, and generate detailed reports on leave trends. By integrating seamlessly with existing HR systems and ensuring secure data management, OverSee helps organizations improve productivity, reduce administrative burdens, and foster a transparent and efficient leave management process.
